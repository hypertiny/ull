The conference. Reinvented.

Úll is a conference built around people. Attendee experience is critical to the planning and design of the event. We value relationships most. We want to maximise serendipity. Our attendees to go away having made new friends, potential partners, useful connections, knowledgeable confidantes.

Úll 2014 takes place in Lyrath Estate, Co. Kilkenny, Ireland. Lyrath is a five star hotel built around the shell of an old Victorian country manor. It features a convention centre, a spa, two restaurants, a café, a foyer, an old house, a games room, a wine bar and 170 acres of parkland.

Let’s face it, sponsorship booths at conference can sometimes be a drab affair. Who really likes being sold things by employees who’d rather be attending the conference?
