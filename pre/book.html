Tickets

We have two types of ticket available, with the option to add accommodation to the core Úll Ticket.

The core Úll ticket is the Úll ticket you know and love. It includes access-everything admission to the conference, which includes the sessions, informal Monday-night dinner, the Úll show, all of the Úll special features on Tuesday, along with lunch, dinner and keynote on Tuesday.

This year, we’re introducing Eagrán, our patronage programme. This ticket covers the complete experience: everything the core ticket includes, plus transport from Dublin, warm-up dinner on Sunday 29th March, 4 nights accommodation (the conference plus one night either side) and wind-down dinner on Wednesday. You’ll get every promotional item that we produce, which have previously included t-shirts, prints and bags, and a special Eagrán-only welcome pack. If that wasn’t already enough, we’ll list your name, or a product or service of your choice, on our site.
