Schedule

The main Úll conference is two days: Tuesday November 1st and Wednesday November 2nd.

The conference days themselves are designed to take advantage of the venue. We start in the afternoon on the first day for two reasons:

- Folks arriving on the day have time to get to Killarney

- Time to take in the resort, go for a walk, take in the natural surroundings

We also organise an informal “day before” and “day after”. We’ve been to many conferences ourselves, and for the most part, we usually arrive a day early and stay another day. By organising a dinner the day before and a wind-down the day after, you can optionally join a bunch of conference-goers ahead of the conference to start things off early, and afterwards, the wind-down day can continue conversations you’ve had, or have a chance to catch up with folks you missed during the two days.
