Speakers: Hand-picked to get the conversations started

Anil Dash

@anildash

Anil Dash is CEO of ThinkUp, a new app that provides deeper insights into our social network. He is also a writer and entrepreneur who focusses on technology affects society. When not doing all of that, he's also an advisor to Medium.

Lauren Goode

@laurengoode

Lauren Goode is a senior reviewer for RE/Code, where she writes about consumer technology, new product developments and technology trends. Lauren previously worked at AllThingsD, The Wall Street Journal and ESPN.

James Thomson

@jamesthomson

James Thomson has been an indie mac developer for over 20 years. He’s the developer for DragThing and PCalc for the Mac, and more recently PCalc for iOS. James has seen his share of frivolous lawsuits and app rejections, and is the subject of at least one Steve Jobs story.

Soroush Khanlou

@khanlou

Soroush Khanlou is the developer of the iOS app for Genius (formerly Rapgenius). He is the author of Markup for iPad. He first took the stage with us last year at our lightning talks session, and then took the main stage at Cocoa Love. We’re delighted to welcome him back as a speaker at Úll this year.

Oisín Prendiville

@prendio2

Oisín Prendiville is an app developer for Supertopsquid, the team behind Castro, Unread and Tokens. Hailing from Ireland, we're delighted to be able to have one of our own. At last year’s Úll, Oisín’s impressive beard meant he was mistaken for Jim Dalrymple.
