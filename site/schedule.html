Schedule

Úll 2015 is March 30 and 31, with the option for attendees to upgrade tickets to include dinner the day before and the day after.

The Conference

Monday 30 March

Keynote sessions and The Úll Show

12pm: Registration opens
2pm–5pm: Talk Sessions
5pm–7pm: Break
7pm: Dinner
9pm: The Úll Show

 

Tuesday 31 March

Talk Sessions, Special Features, Keynote, Banquet

10am–1pm: Talk Sessions
1pm–3pm: Lunch
3pm–6pm: Special Features
6pm–8pm: Wind-down
8pm: Dinner, followed by keynote, followed by music

Extend Your Stay

Before: Sunday 29 March

Early arrivals the day before. Dinner is included in Eagrán tickets, and available as an optional upgrade for regular tickets.

8pm: Dinner

After: Wednesday 1 April

For some folks, the wind-down is as important as the conference itself! Also included in Eagrán tickets, dinner is again available as an upgrade for regular tickets.
